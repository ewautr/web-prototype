WHAT IS WORK SPACE?

It is an online platform that allows you to contact your teammates, share files with them, create to do lists, view task descriptions, record screencasts and much more.

TRY KEA'S INTRANET CHAT

In our chat section you can contact any KEA's student and teacher is a easy and user-friendly way.

ASK TEACHERS FOR HELP

One of Work Space's most valued features is the option to ask your teacher for help with your assignment at any point in time. After your team gets procious advice and finishes the assignment you can easily submit it directly form the platform.
